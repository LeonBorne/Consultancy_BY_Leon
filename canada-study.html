Why study in Canada?

  • Canada consistently ranks as one of the best countries for quality of life, making it an attractive destination for Nepali students seeking an excellent education.
  • The country is home to world-renowned universities and colleges that offer a wide range of programs tailored to meet global market demands.
  • Tuition fees in Canada are competitive compared to other countries, combined with opportunities for scholarships and financial aid for international students.
  • Canada’s multicultural society provides a safe and inclusive environment, allowing students from various backgrounds to feel at home.
  • Canadian degrees are recognized worldwide, greatly enhancing your employability and career prospects post-graduation.
  • The emphasis on research and hands-on learning ensures that you gain applicable skills and experience
  • With a focus on student well-being, universities in Canada offer comprehensive support services, including counseling and academic resources.

Study in Canada : Step By Step Process

  1. Shortlist your preferred course and university
  2. Prepare academic documents and take English proficiency tests (IELTS/PTE)
  3. Submit your application and wait for the offer letter
  4. Arrange funds and pay initial fees to receive a Letter of Acceptance (LoA)
  5. Complete medical examinations and obtain health insurance
  6. Submit your study permit application and wait for approval
  7. Prepare for departure: accommodation, flights, and orientation
Abroad Bridge Consultancy offers assistance at every stage — from choosing the right course to visa approval.
